About me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Oxford. I have three years experience in programming: my main expertise is with Python though I am also familiar with Java and web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I have recently published an Android app to the Google Play store.

Charades Prompts App

I recently published the first iteration of an app on the Google Play Store which presents users with a selection of categories. Upon selection they are presented with a series of prompts to act out as in the classic party game. Importantly, the layout of the app changes to reflect both the size of the mobile device and whether it is held in either a portrait or landscape mode.

PyNonbonded

My most important project was completed for my Masters thesis in Computational Chemistry. The project sought to describe forces between molecules using a forcefield-style set of empirical potentials. The source code is available from the GitHub repository and we also deployed a version to the cloud using Google App Engine which is hosted here.
